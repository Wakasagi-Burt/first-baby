About the Creators

We are a group of four students currently enrolled in Georgia Techs' Full Stack Bootcamp. We created Film Detective for our first project using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript code. Why did we choose this website? Well we all share an interest in movies and wanted to help you find a movie you might like from all over the globe and a way to store your movies in a watchlist so that you do not have to continually look up the title. We hope you enjoy the very first stages of our website!
